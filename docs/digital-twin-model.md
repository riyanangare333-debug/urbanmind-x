# Digital Twin Model

## UrbanMind-X Digital Twin Framework

UrbanMind-X is built around a Digital Twin Architecture that creates a dynamic virtual representation of campus infrastructure, population movement, resource systems, and operational behavior.

The digital twin continuously models, simulates, and evaluates the state of the campus ecosystem to support intelligent decision-making.

---

# What is a Digital Twin?

A digital twin is a virtual representation of a physical environment that mirrors real-world systems, processes, and interactions.

UrbanMind-X uses digital twins to:

- Monitor infrastructure
- Simulate future states
- Forecast demand
- Optimize operations
- Test policies before deployment
