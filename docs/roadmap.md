# Roadmap

## UrbanMind-X Development Roadmap

UrbanMind-X is being developed as a Digital Twin Intelligence Platform capable of transforming campuses into autonomous and self-optimizing micro-cities through simulation, predictive analytics, and AI-driven decision intelligence.

---

Status: Completed

Objectives:

- Digital twin architecture design
- Infrastructure modeling framework
- Smart campus problem analysis
- Simulation architecture planning
- Optimization framework design

Deliverables:

- UrbanMind-X Concept Architecture
- Digital Twin Model
- Infrastructure Intelligence Framework
- Research Documentation
