# Technology Stack

## UrbanMind-X Technology Architecture

UrbanMind-X combines digital twin modeling, simulation systems, predictive AI, and optimization intelligence into a scalable infrastructure analytics platform.

The technology stack is designed to support real-time infrastructure modeling, autonomous decision intelligence, and future city-scale deployment.
